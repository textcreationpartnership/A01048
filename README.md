##Content Summary##

#####Front#####

1. To the Rightly right Honorable Lady,
the Lady Penelope, Countesse
of Deuonshire.

1. To eache affected Reader.

1. To Master Iohn Forde of the Middle
Temple, vp on his Fames Memoriall,
this Madrigall.

1. In eundem.

#####Body#####

1. THE
EARLE of Deuonshire
deceased.

    _ His Tombe
The Epitaph.

    _ Anagramma ex Camdeno.
CAROLVS BLVNTVS.
Bonus, vt sol clarus.
